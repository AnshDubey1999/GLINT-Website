Reasoning

Why was this developed?

I thought it would be cool and helpful for someone to have all those details on a single platform, i.e., visit this website first, find the correct movie and see if its something they want to watch or no and from there get linked directly to Netflix...if not, it's a good side project!

Distinct features

Can't Google or IMDB also do this?

Yes, it can but you need to know what you want to watch there; however, on this website, you can browse through many shows to make up your mind and then go get linked to Netflix

Functionality

What does this website do?

New Releases, Expiring soon, Season changes, details like rating, genre, plot, etc. all on one platform :) Isn't that amazing?!
